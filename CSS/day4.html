Important Notice

All students are hereby informed that our annual College Arts Festival 2026 will be held from 10 August to 12 August 2026 on the college campus. The festival aims to provide students with a platform to showcase their creativity, artistic talents, and cultural spirit. A variety of events have been planned, including music, dance, drama, painting, photography, literary competitions, fashion shows, and various cultural performances. Students who wish to participate are requested to register with their respective department coordinators on or before 31 July 2026. Participants may register for individual as well as group events, subject to the festival guidelines. Detailed schedules, event rules, and venue information will be displayed on the college notice board and shared through the official student communication channels. Participants are advised to attend the orientation meeting on 5 August 2026 at 10:00 AM in the college auditorium, where event coordinators will explain the competition rules, judging criteria, reporting times, and other important instructions. All participants are expected to be punctual and maintain discipline throughout the festival. The festival will also feature exhibitions, food stalls, workshops, and guest performances, providing students with an opportunity to interact, learn, and celebrate together. Winners of each event will receive trophies, certificates, and exciting prizes during the closing ceremony. All students are encouraged to participate enthusiastically and contribute to making the festival a memorable celebration of creativity, teamwork, and cultural diversity. Your active involvement will ensure the success of this grand event and strengthen the vibrant spirit of our college community.
