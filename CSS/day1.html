Price: Approximately ₹49,990

The laptop comes with Windows 11 Home and a variety of ports for connectivity. It is suitable for multitasking, web browsing, and running productivity applications. The Dell Inspiron 15 3530 is a great choice for those looking for a balance between performance and affordability.

Overall, the Dell Inspiron 15 3530 is a solid option for students and professionals who need a dependable laptop for their daily tasks. Its combination of performance, storage, and display quality makes it a versatile choice for various computing needs.
